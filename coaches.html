Coaches

Ivan Ivanov

Boxing coach

Meet Ivan Ivanov, the seasoned boxing coach at IronFist. Hailing from the rugged streets of Sofia, Bulgaria, Ivan discovered his passion for boxing at a young age. Enduring tough times, he sought refuge in the discipline and artistry of the sport.

Ivan competed at the national level, earning respect for his lightning-fast jabs and unyielding determination. After hanging up his gloves, Ivan found a new purpose at IronFist, where he imparts his invaluable knowledge to aspiring pugilists.

With a keen eye for technique and a heart dedicated to the sweet science, Ivan guides his students to victory, both inside and outside the ring.

Georgi Petrov

Conditioning coach

Enter Georgi Petrov, the powerhouse behind IronFist's conditioning program. Growing up in the heart of Varna, Bulgaria, Georgi's journey to fitness started early. A former decathlete, he represented his country in international competitions, showcasing his prowess in strength, speed, and endurance.

Georgi's passion for sculpting peak physical performance brought him to IronFist, where he tailors cutting-edge conditioning routines for fighters of all levels.

His commitment to pushing boundaries ensures that IronFist athletes not only survive but thrive in the grueling world of combat sports.

Dimitar Angelov

Kickboxing coach

Introducing Dimitar Angelov, the dynamic kickboxing coach at IronFist. Born and raised in the vibrant city of Plovdiv, Bulgaria, Dimitar's journey into kickboxing began as a quest for self-discipline.

A former national kickboxing champion, he seamlessly blends traditional techniques with modern strategies, creating a unique coaching style. Dimitar's commitment to empowering his students goes beyond the ring, instilling confidence and resilience.

At IronFist, he continues to shape formidable kickboxers, channeling his passion for the sport into a powerful force that resonates with every high kick and precision strike.

Martin Dimitrov

Boxing coach

Meet Martin Dimitrov, the precision-driven boxing coach at IronFist. Born and raised in Sofia, Bulgaria, Martin's journey into the world of boxing began as a teenager, inspired by the timeless elegance of the sport. His dedication led him to regional boxing championships, where he honed his craft and developed a deep understanding of the sweet science.

Martin's approach to coaching is rooted in the technical nuances of boxing, emphasizing footwork, timing, and the perfect execution of each punch. At IronFist, he imparts his passion for the art of boxing to a new generation of fighters, instilling not just physical prowess but also a profound respect for the discipline and tradition of the sport.

Martin's commitment to excellence makes him a cornerstone of the IronFist coaching team, shaping boxers who are not only formidable in the ring but also carry the spirit of true sportsmanship.
