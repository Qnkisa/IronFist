Kickboxing

Kickboxing, a potent striking martial art, employs a seamless fusion of punches, kicks, knees, and elbows for effective long-range combat while standing. At IronFirst, our emphasis is on refining stance, technique, and the execution of fluid combinations. Each session concludes with a challenging circuit, allowing you to push your cardiovascular limits to the fullest.

Boxing

Boxing is a combat sport involving fist-to-fist encounters, where you engage in a dynamic 'dance' with your opponent. At IronFist, we prioritize the development of footwork and the proficiency to absorb, evade, and execute seamless punching combinations.

Functional training
